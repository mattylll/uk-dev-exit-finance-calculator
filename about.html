About

About this calculator

What this is

A single-purpose calculator for UK property developers nearing practical completion who are comparing development exit finance against a term refinance. Numbers compute in your browser, nothing is sent anywhere, no account required.

Who writes this

Calculator logic, defaults and editorial by Olivia Chen, CA, cash flow analyst for property developers. Olivia is a qualified chartered accountant working with UK property developers on cash flow modelling through the sales period. Specialises in worked examples and break-even analysis.

Editorial independence

This site is not a broker and not a lender. We don't collect your inputs and we don't use them for anything. If you want live indicative terms on an actual scheme, the fastest route is a UK broker with a current dev exit lender panel — we occasionally point readers at Construction Capital . They have no editorial input here.

Corrections

If a default looks wrong, or the methodology misses something material on a specific structure, let us know. The calculator improves fastest with developer feedback.
